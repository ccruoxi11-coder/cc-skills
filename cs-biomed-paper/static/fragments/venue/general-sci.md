# Venue: General SCI / IF journal (default)

Use when the user names a specialty or general SCI journal that isn't Nature-family, a flagship IEEE
technical journal, or an ML conference.

## Framing

- Standard **IMRaD**, balanced between clinical/biological relevance and method.
- Match the **specific journal's** scope, audience, and article-type rules — confirm them rather than
  assuming (principle: primary source). Read the journal's *Author Guidelines* for word/figure limits,
  structured-abstract format, and citation style.
- Reporting-standard adherence and data/code availability are increasingly required across SCI
  journals; assume they're needed unless the guidelines say otherwise.

## Mechanics

- Follow the journal's template, reference style, and structured-abstract requirement.
- Cover letter stating fit and significance; suggested reviewers if requested.

## Pitfalls

Writing generically without checking the target journal's guidelines; wrong article type; mismatched
scope; ignoring the journal's reporting-standard/data policy. Confirm the target:
`[待补充：目标期刊及作者须知链接]`.
