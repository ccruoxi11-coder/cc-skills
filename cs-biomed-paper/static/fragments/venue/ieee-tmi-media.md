# Venue: IEEE TMI / Medical Image Analysis / TBME / JBHI (technical journals)

## Framing

- **Methodological rigor and novelty are primary**, but clinical relevance and validation are
  expected — these venues increasingly demand external validation and reporting-standard adherence.
- Full IMRaD with a detailed Methods; thorough experiments, ablations, and comparison to current SOTA
  on recognized benchmarks.
- Reproducibility valued: code release, exact settings, statistical comparison across methods.

## Mechanics

- Longer page allowance than conferences; detailed math and supplementary experiments fit.
- Strong, current baselines on standard datasets are expected; significance testing across methods.
- Figures to IEEE spec; double-column layout constrains figure width.

## Pitfalls

Insufficient comparison to recent methods; no significance test; clinical validation treated as
optional; missing reproducibility detail. Confirm the exact journal: `[待补充：目标期刊]`.
