# Venue: MICCAI / CVPR / NeurIPS / ICLR (ML conferences)

## Framing

- **Methodological novelty and empirical strength lead.** A clear contribution, strong baselines, and
  convincing ablations matter most; clinical depth is lighter than in medical journals but reviewers
  still check leakage and validation.
- Hard page limit → tight, figure-dense writing; details pushed to supplementary/appendix.
- Reproducibility checklists are often mandatory (e.g. NeurIPS reproducibility checklist); anonymize
  for double-blind review.

## Mechanics

- Strict template, page limit, and deadline; supplementary for extra experiments.
- Code/reproducibility statement expected; many venues require a checklist at submission.
- MICCAI specifically still expects sound medical-data handling (patient-level splits, proper metrics).

## Pitfalls

Over-length; weak/old baselines; ablations that don't isolate the contribution; de-anonymization leaks;
gains within noise without seed variance; ignoring patient-level splits at MICCAI. Confirm venue +
deadline + blind policy: `[待补充：会议 / 截稿 / 盲审要求]`.
