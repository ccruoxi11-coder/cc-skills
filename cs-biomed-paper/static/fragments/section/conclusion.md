# Section: Conclusion

## Rules

- One tight paragraph: **contribution + key evidence + impact + boundary**.
- No new results, no new citations.
- Match the claim to what was actually demonstrated (including the validation scope).
- End on the bounded next step or implication, not a hype sentence.

## Pitfalls

- Introducing data or claims not in Results.
- Re-inflating the claim the Discussion just bounded.
- Vague "this opens new avenues" with no concrete direction.

Keep it short; if the venue folds conclusions into the Discussion, say so.
